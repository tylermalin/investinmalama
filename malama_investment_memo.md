## THE OPPORTUNITY

### **Market Context: The Trust Gap in Carbon Credits**

The voluntary carbon market (VCM) is projected to grow from **$887B (2025) → $1.22T (2026) → $5T+ (2030)**, driven by:

1. **Institutional flight to quality:** Microsoft, Amazon, Google demanding high-integrity durable removal (not cheap offsets)
2. **Regulatory tailwinds:** SEC climate disclosure requirements, Article 6.4 compliance standards, ISO 18100-series formalization
3. **Technology maturation:** Blockchain + IoT + AI creating real-time, auditable MRV (monitoring, reporting, verification)

**The Problem:** Traditional carbon verification takes 12–18 months, costs $20K–$50K per project, and relies on manual third-party audits. This creates:
- **Trust gap:** Buyers skeptical of credit integrity (legacy offset fraud)
- **Speed gap:** Project developers can't presell future credits (no early liquidity)
- **Quality gap:** High-integrity credits ($80–150/tCO₂e) sold alongside commodity offsets ($10–20/tCO₂e)

**Mālama's Solution:** Decentralized MRV (dMRV) platform combining:
- **Blockchain-anchored proof** (every data point signed, timestamped, immutable on Cardano)
- **AI risk engine** (predicts CO₂ removal with 95%+ confidence in Phase 1, justifying presale tokens)
- **Integrated methodology** (ERW + biochar + soil carbon = 3× removal per dollar)
- **Landowner alignment** ($303K/acre value sharing = 5–10 year projects stick)
